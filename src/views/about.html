Background

My name is Harrison Merrill, and I am a Junior in Applied Technology, with an emphasis on web development, Database Design, and Information Security. I have been working on or with computers with my dad in some capacity since the windows 95 days, but have mostly just done things for fun or to troubleshoot a specific problem; this did however help guide me into deciding to get a degree in technology. I took a 6-year hiatus from school after my oldest child was born for several reasons, but now that he is in school himself, I started coming back to BYU-I last year.

For my fun fact, I live in Japan, where I have lived for the last 8 years, and currently live in Okinawa though I spent most of my time in Japan in the greater Tokyo area in Tachikawa. This is because my wife works for the Air Force as a maintainer (mechanic) on C-130J's. For fun, when I find time that isn't dominated by school, family, or community, I like to play games like board games, video games, and tabletop games, cook or barbecue, and paint miniature figurines.

Web development has turned into the main focus of my degree program, and while I've done a lot with various languages and front-end things, this will be my first back-end course. I'm hoping that by the end of this semester, I will become a confident full-stack developer.
